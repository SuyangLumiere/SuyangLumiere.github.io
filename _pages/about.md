Hi there! 👋 I am an M.S. student in Computer Science at **Central South University (CSU)**. 

I have a unique and highly diverse background. My academic journey began in **Finance and Big Data Management** at ZUEL, which equipped me with strong logical reasoning and macro-level strategic thinking. Driven by a passion for complex data, I transitioned into bioinformatics, specifically **Spatial Transcriptomics**, where I honed my skills in processing high-dimensional biological datasets.

Today, I channel all these interdisciplinary analytical skills into the frontiers of Artificial Intelligence. I am fully dedicated to **Computer Vision (CV)** and **Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLM)**. I firmly believe my non-traditional path is my superpower, allowing me to approach AI research and problem-solving from fresh, multifaceted perspectives!

Feel free to reach out for collaborations, discussions, or just a quick chat about AI!

**I am currently looking for research internship opportunities in the field of MLLM. Feel free to drop me an email!**
